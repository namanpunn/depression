About Depression

Depression is a serious and common medical psychological condition that affects the way how you think, feel, and act. It interferes with your abilities and makes it difficult for you to do your everyday tasks.

It is influenced by many things. Changes in the brain, like imbalances in chemicals, can contribute. Hormones and genetics also play a part.
