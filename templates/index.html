Welcome to Healing Neuro

Welcome to Healing Neuro, where we harness the power of machine learning
technology to identify the individuals who may be experiencing depression.

textual responses to understand the emotional state and well-being of our users.
Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive space for individuals to express
themselves honestly and openly, knowing that their responses will be met with
understanding and compassion.
