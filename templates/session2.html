READY TO BE IN SITUATIONS..

  • 1.Think about a recent conflict you've had with someone close to you. What triggered the conflict,
    and how did you handle it?

  • 2.Picture yourself in a situation where you're feeling overwhelmed or anxious. What thoughts or emotions tend to arise, and how do you usually cope with them?

  • 3."If you could change one thing about your current situation, what would it be and why?"


  • 4.Consider a goal or aspiration you've been struggling to achieve.
    What obstacles have you encountered, and what steps have you taken to overcome them?"

  • 5. "Imagine you're faced with a major life transition, such as starting a new job or moving to a new city.
    How do you typically adapt to change?"

  • 6.Reflect on a time when you felt particularly fulfilled or satisfied. What were you doing,
    and what made that experience meaningful to you?
